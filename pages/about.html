Best Noodle Shop in Boston!

Dan Dan Noodles was founded in December 2024 in a small apartment in Jamaica Plain, MA, where the magic first began. Inspired by Japanese cuisine and a passion for food, Dan Dan Noodles was born. This restaurant isn’t just about eating—it’s about indulging, to the point where you might need to unbutton a button or two. You’ve heard of large noodle bowls, but have you ever encountered XXXL Noodle Bowls? These massive creations will leave you so full that you won’t even think about eating the next morning. With your first taste of our homemade broth, you’ll be transported back to childhood, savoring Campbell’s Chicken Noodle Soup during a Boston blizzard—but imagine that experience 100 times better! Our noodle bowls are made to order, and every bite is crafted with care. However, if you can’t finish your bowl, we may have to (politely) ask you not to come back. After all, at Dan Dan Noodles, it’s all about going big—or going home!

About the Chef

          Thuong grew up eating instant ramen noodle packets during the early 2000s. To this day, he still enjoys them but has elevated their flavor by adding whatever ingredients he can find in the fridge. His inspiration comes from cookbooks, YouTube videos, and social media. Thuong earned a Culinary Certification from Bunker Hill Community College in Charlestown, MA, and has won numerous awards and heartfelt praise from his loving partner. The decision of who ranks #1 in their eyes is uncontested—it's Thuong. If not for his current career, Thuong would undoubtedly be a top chef at Dan Dan Noodles, earning more than anyone could imagine.
